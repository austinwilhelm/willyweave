in this age of quiet anxiety  
a shudder or a screech  
feeling lost or in between  
not sure where to go  
who to ask next  
  
for there's no place like home  
when you're lost at sea  
waves and storm of your mind thrash  
longing to be home somewhere  
feeling something whole again  

-aw
