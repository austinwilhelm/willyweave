not sure where i am  
where to begin  
or where i seem to belong  
  
peaceful feelings receding  
time fading and passes all the same  
who am i to change a trajectory  
  
never pity me  
for i know what i'm capable of  
only wishing others could see it  
and feel it  


-aw
