wars and waging  
taxes and void  
for what else may we do  
while this world gets destroyed  

love in your words  
and hate in your hearts  
this world needs change  
care cannot be this sparse    
  
a God of mythical proportion  
keeps many at bay  
yet the earth that we see  
is the reason we stay  

  
-aw
