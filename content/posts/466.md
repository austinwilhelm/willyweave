a mother to never return home  
a daughter to never hold again  
a neighbor to never wave back  
  
to what cruelty  
do we owe these immense feelings  
of hatred and anger  
  
boiling within us  
all the same darkness  
that only light can quell  
  
we must bring that light forth  

**rest in power Renee Nicole Good**
