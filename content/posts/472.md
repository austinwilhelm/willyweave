we met in the middle  
the middle of this ugly time  
the middle of a continent  
in the midst of tragedy  
  
yet something stirs  
deep within me  
inspiring tides of warmth  
to wash over me entirely  
  
as we met in the middle  
this magical compromise  

-aw
