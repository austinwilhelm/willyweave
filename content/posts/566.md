the waves have come  
crashed upon my shore  
left me sullen and alone  
tearing apart my rapport   
  
for i miss thee well  
it's been quite some time  
just one more call with you  
would be more than sublime  

in each others orbit  
for if only a fraction of life  
oh how i wish you gave into love  
instead of into strife  
  
-aw
