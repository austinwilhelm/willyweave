do I choose chaos?  
do I dare subject myself to the fire?  
will it mold me, or will it leave me formless  
is that up to me?  
  
when do I know enough to decide?  
that uninhibited limit, that boundary  
is it contrived, is it judgmental  
am I wrong?  

-aw
