so much to learn and gain  
on the other side of fear and discomfort  
the great smokescreen  
keeping us locked in our rooms of comfort  
  
alas here i go  
a note to you old friend  
for i will never be the same  
after these events unfold  
  
or any events for that matter  

-aw
