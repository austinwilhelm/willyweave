dear friend  
i see the hurt inside you  
from whatever it was  
long long ago  

the protective shell  
conception from fear  
it's not your fault  
it's not your fault  
  
there is no cost  
too sunk to turn back from  
get back to your heart  
you'll know right and wrong  

-aw
