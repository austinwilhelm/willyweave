to what do i owe the pleasure  
an inward facing eye  
a brain stem that fights to feel  
do i fight to feel?  
  
looking back in love, care, kind  
cherishing, grateful, happy  
sad that it's come and gone   
but forever happy it came  
  
at all  

-aw
