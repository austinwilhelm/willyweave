these quiet moments  
devoid of noise, of fear  
a glimpse, a shimmer  
  
wrapped up in this embryonic idealism  
what kind of pipe dream  
have i let myself consume again and again  
  
what have i let myself believe  
firing together, wiring together  
we're going to be alright  
 
we can meet again somewhere  
somewhere far away from here  

-aw
