a silver box  
filled with my fears  
the darkness of a room  
both haunting and guiding  
  
closing my eyes  
may not have meaning  
yet i sink into a pool
of infinite boundless waves  

letting go  
expanding  
seeking the beyond  
just another tuesday afternoon
